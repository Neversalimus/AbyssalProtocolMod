# Abyssal Protocol

**Abyssal Protocol** is a RimWorld techno-demonic endgame mod centered around controlled summoning, abyssal progression, and custom boss encounters.

Instead of being just another content pack with stronger gear, the mod is built around a deliberate escalation loop: research forbidden technologies, construct infernal infrastructure, craft ritual components, trigger abyssal encounters, survive them, and turn the rewards into stronger weapons, implants, and future summons.

## Current Project State

**Stage:** Pre-Alpha / First Vertical Slice

Abyssal Protocol is already past the pure concept phase.

The current build contains a playable foundation of the mod’s intended identity:
- a custom summoning workbench pipeline
- early abyssal research and crafting
- a working boss summon flow
- custom hostile entities
- portal-based encounter escalation
- early custom weapons and implants
- custom art, VFX-style content, and audio support
- English and Russian localization

This is not a full 1.0 content-complete release yet, but it is already a real playable vertical slice with dedicated systems and custom code.

## Core Idea

Abyssal Protocol is designed as a **summon-driven infernal progression mod**.

You do not simply research top-tier gear and craft your way to power.  
Instead, progression is meant to be earned through increasingly dangerous abyssal encounters.

The intended loop is:

1. Unlock abyssal technologies.
2. Build infernal infrastructure.
3. Refine ritual materials.
4. Craft summon components.
5. Trigger controlled hostile encounters.
6. Defeat abyssal entities.
7. Convert rewards into stronger gear and deeper progression.

The goal is to create a high-threat, high-reward late-game content path with a strong identity: infernal, industrial, ritualized, and hostile.

## What Makes The Mod Different

Abyssal Protocol is not meant to feel like “generic demons” or “just another overpowered faction”.

Its style is built around:
- infernal symbolism fused with high technology
- industrial ritual machinery
- abyssal bio-mechanical entities
- controlled summon encounters instead of passive content
- boss-oriented progression with strong presentation

The intended tone is closer to **forbidden ultra-tech ritual warfare** than to traditional fantasy demon content.

## Current Gameplay Focus

Right now, development is focused on establishing the first strong gameplay pillar of the mod:

- a reliable summon pipeline
- a memorable first boss encounter
- portal/phase escalation logic
- stronger audiovisual identity

In other words, the current version is about proving the core loop works in-game — not just on paper.
