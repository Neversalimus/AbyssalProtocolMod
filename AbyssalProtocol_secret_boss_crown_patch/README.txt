Abyssal Protocol secret boss reward patch

What this patch adds
- Keeps the working first boss reward (Archon Override Ampoule) with sequenced positive/negative effect.
- Adds a secret boss reward for ABY_ArchonOfRupture: Crown of Rupture.
- The crown is a unique ultra-tech relic weapon with one Rupture Sentence shot every 24 in-game hours.
- The shot deals high damage, very high armor penetration, and applies a short debuff mark.

Install
- Extract this archive into the root of your mod folder.
- Allow file replacement.
